Risks of Lung Transplant

Extended Hospital Stay

Complications after the lung transplant can sometimes require you to stay in the hospital for a length of time immediately after surgery. This can be due to several different problems that the doctors want to monitor. It is likely that with a complication, you would stay 3-5 days in the Intensive Care Unit, followed by a month in the hospital afterwards. You would be required to live within 2 hours distance from the hospital for two to three months after you leave.

2+ month stay

Out of 100 patients like you, 16 will have a one-month hospital stay after surgery. 5 will have a hospital stay of 2 or more months.
