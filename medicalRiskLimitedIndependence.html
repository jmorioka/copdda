Treatment Options’ Risks

Treating COPD medically, you can’t be entirely independent.

Depending on how sick you are, you may still need to depend on others to shop, cook, clean, drive, etc. for you. You’ll also still need someone who can look after you and take you to the doctor or the hospital when you need to go.

Out of 100 patients like you, about 90 of them will still have limited independence treating COPD medically.
