What is COPD?

COPD stands for Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease. Here is what each word in that name means:

  • Chronic: long-lasting
  • Obstructive: based on the word obstacleâ€”something that creates blockages
  • Pulmonary: related to lungs
  • Disease: a medical condition
Though the name can be confusing, once you know what each word means, it makes sense. COPD is a long-lasting disease that creates blockages in your lungs, making it difficult to breathe.

As the video above explains, COPD is one word for two diseases. People with COPD have both emphysema and chronic bronchitis to some degree.
