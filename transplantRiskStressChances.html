Risks of Lung Transplant

Stressful Experience for You and Your Caregivers

It is a huge commitment for your caregiver in terms of time and work they need to put in. Some caregivers for lung transplant patients say they still have nightmares about it years after the patient gets a transplant.

Out of all caregivers we surveyed, 50% said it was extremely stressful. 20% of all caregivers have so much stress from the process that they step down from their role as caregiver.
