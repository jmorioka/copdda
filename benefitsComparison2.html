Benefits of Medical Treatment vs. Lung Transplant

Benefits of Medical Treatment Benefits of Lung Transplant
You have more control over the success of your treatment. You will probably be able to be able to go without oxygen.
Medical treatment of COPD is usually less expensive than getting a lung transplant (though it does depend on your insurance.) You will probably be able to get around more. You could walk your dog or go on a walk with a friend, be able go back to work or volunteering, or even play sports.
The medicines you are taking aren't as bad for the rest of your body as the medicines you would have to take for transplant. You could be more independent. You could be able to drive again, to cook for yourself, and do house or yard work on your own.
Helping you with medical treatment can be much easier for the people taking care of you than going through the process of helping you get a transplant. You can spend time with family and friends and be more able to keep up (especially with grandkids.)
