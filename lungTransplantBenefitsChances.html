Lung Transplant Surgery: Chances of Benefits

Go without oxygen

Out of 100 COPD patients who get lung transplants, about 90 of them will be able to go off oxygen for 2 years after transplant.

Get around more

Out of 100 COPD patients who get lung transplants, about 92 of them say they are “significantly more active after the transplant” than before the transplant.

Independence

Out of 100 COPD patients who get lung transplants, about 91 of the patients will drive after the transplant and 83 of the COPD patients who get lung transplants reported “relying on caregivers for basic needs”.

Keep up better

Out of 100 COPD patients who get lung transplants, about 86 of them reported being able to “keep up better with family and friends" after the transplant.

Travel

Out of 100 COPD patients who get lung transplants, about 67 of the patients reported that “travel is easier” and 48 of the 100 COPD patients who get lung transplants like you traveled by air in past year.
