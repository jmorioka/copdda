Lung Transplant Surgery: Chances of Benefiting from Lung Transplant Surgery

Going without oxygen

Out of 100 patients like you, about 90 of them will be able to go off oxygen for 2 years after transplant.

Get Around More

Out of 100 patients like you, about 92 of them say they are “significantly more active after the transplant” than before the transplant.

Being more independent

Out of 100 patients like you, about 91 of the patients can drive after the transplant and 83 of the patients report “decreased reliance on caregivers for basic needs”.

Keeping up with family and friends

Out of 100 patients like you, about 86 of them report being able to “keep up better with family and friends" after the transplant.

Traveling

Out of 100 patients like you, about 67 of the patients report that “travel is easier” and 48 of the 100 patients like you travelled by air in past year.
