Chances of Experiencing Risks of Lung Transplant

Stressful to get a transplant

Out of 100 patients like you, 97 patients whose caregivers report that the transplant process was “severely stressful”.

Out of 100 patients like you, 88 patients whose caregivers report spending 6 hours/week before transplant working on logistics of the transplant process.

Might never get lungs

Out of 100 patients like you, 28 patients listed for transplant don’t get lungs for transplant. At UPMC, 23 patients out of 100 patients like you don't get lungs for transplant.

Hallucinations

Out of 100 patients like you, about 74 of patients experience hallucinations while at hospital after the transplant.

Out of 100 patients like you, about 89 experience tremors when taking post transplant drugs.

Being on a ventilator

Out of 100 patients like you, about 10 of them will be on a ventilator right after transplant.

Extended hospital stay

Out of 100 patients like you, about 90 of them will be able to go off oxygen for a period of time after transplant.

Rejection

Out of 100 patients like you, about 90 of them will be able to go off oxygen for a period of time after transplant.

Trading One Disease for Another

Out of 100 patients like you, about 90 of them will be able to go off oxygen for a period of time after transplant.
