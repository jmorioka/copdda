COPD Decision Aid

A tool for education and reflection for patients with Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease who are considering their treatment options.

Who is this website for?

This website and the text, pictures, and videos on it are meant to inform patients with severe Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) (What is COPD?) about their treatment options. It focuses especially on the choice between managing COPD using medicines and getting a lung transplant.

How to navigate

To navigate between pages on this website, use the arrow keys on your keyboard. The right arrow key will move you forward through the pages of the website, and the left arrow key will move you back (to the previous page you viewed). On any page, you can press the "H" key to hear the text on the page read aloud. On some pages, there will be more instructions on how to use the keys on the keyboard to use the website.
